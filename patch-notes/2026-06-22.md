**🛠️ Patch Notes**

**🏆 SFRanks — Earn Your Place on the Ladder**

A brand-new earned-rank system, driven by your **total skill levels** across all six SFRpg skills (Digging + Mining + Logging + Gardening + Hunting + Fishing). Every 100 total levels you cross unlocks the next rank — and bigger perks.

**The ladder** (low → high):
- **Rookie** — 0+ total skill levels
- **Warrior** — 100+
- **Spartan** — 200+
- **General** — 300+
- **Demigod** — 400+
- **Olympian** — 500+
- **Titan** — 600+
- **Immortal** — 700+
- **Goat** — 800+

**Per-rank perks** (more to come over time):
- **+1 home per rankup.** Rookies keep the existing 3 homes; you climb all the way to **11 homes at Goat**.
- **Auto Sell Chests placed at once** — Rookies can have 1, +1 every 2 ranks, capped at **5 at Goat**.
- **Pandora's Chests placed at once** — same scaling: 1 → 5.

**What you'll see in-game:**
- A colored `[Rank]` tag in **chat** (between your role/title and your name) and in the **tab list**.
- Hitting a new rank fires a server-wide announcement, a title pop on your screen, and a celebratory sound.
- New command **`/rank`** — your current rank, progress to the next, and your current perks.
- **`/rank ladder`** — the full ladder with thresholds and perks at each tier.
- **`/rank <player>`** — look up another player.

> ⚠️ **If you already have 100+ total skill levels, you'll be credited your earned rank the next time you log in** — no action required. The announcement and sound will play once for your final earned rank (no spam for each tier you skipped past).

> ⚠️ **Already over a chest cap?** Anything you've already placed stays placed and works exactly as before. You just can't place a *new* Auto Sell or Pandora's Chest of that type until your placed count drops below your rank's cap (pick one up, or rank up).

**🌐 SFChat — Website, Discord, and Vote Links**
- **`/website`** (alias **`/site`**) is now usable by every member and shows the link to **just you** — no more broadcasting.
- New **`/discord`** command shows you a clickable link to join the Sleeping Forest Discord at **discord.gg/ApUMWj4vK3**.
- New **`/broadcastsite`** and **`/broadcastdiscord`** commands (admin only) broadcast the website and Discord links to every online player. The old `/website` broadcast behaviour now lives under `/broadcastsite`.
- **`/vote`** (anyone) still shows a private clickable link to vote for Sleeping Forest on Planet Minecraft.

---

**💬 SFChat — Pink, Orange, and Light Green Readability Fix**
- The **Pink**, **Orange**, and **Light Green** options in `/chat color` were previously pulled from Minecraft's wool/dye palette so they'd match the dye icon. Those exact RGBs turned out to be hard to read against chat backgrounds.
- All three now use the closest vanilla chat color instead — brighter and far more legible:
  - **Pink** → vanilla Light Purple
  - **Orange** → vanilla Gold
  - **Light Green** → vanilla Lime
- If you'd already picked one of those three colors, your selection is automatically bumped to the new color the next time you log in — no need to re-open the picker.

---

**⛏️ Vein Miner — Now Gives Full Mining XP**
- **Bug fix:** Vein Miner was only crediting Mining XP for the *first* block you clicked — every other ore in the vein was broken silently with no XP.
- Now every ore Vein Miner breaks gives the same XP it would if you'd mined it by hand (Coal/Copper **5**, Iron **10**, Gold/Emerald **25**, Diamond **50**, other ores **1**).
- Silk Touch still skips XP, same as a regular mine.

**🛒 SFShop — Brand-New Shop GUI**
The shop is now a full GUI experience. Every physical sign you've already placed still works exactly as before.

- `/shop` now opens a **4-row menu** with Buy / Sell / Teleport buttons and a **Recent Purchases band** showing your last 5 buys — click any of them to instantly repurchase the same item and quantity at the current price.
- Buy and Sell browse by category: **Blocks, Redstone, Decoration, Ores, Spawners, Misc**. Only items with shop signs appear in each tab.
- Clicking a buy item opens a **quantity prompt**: pick a preset (**1 / 16 / 32 / 64**) or use the red/lime glass panes to dial in any custom amount. **Capped at 10 stacks** per transaction.
- Costs you can't afford are shown in **red** and clicking them does nothing — no more accidental "Insufficient funds" spam in chat.
- Spawners get their own tab and display as the **actual spawner item** you receive (correct spawn-egg model and name).

> ⚠️ **`/shop` no longer teleports as its default action.** Use the new **Teleport to Shop** button inside the GUI.

---

**💰 SFShop — New `/sell` Command**
- `/sell` instantly sells every plain item in your inventory that has an admin **Sell** sign anywhere on the server, at the best per-unit price available.
- Custom-named tools, enchanted items, mending books, custom-enchant books, and spawners are **never** auto-sold — safe to run with valuable items in your inventory.
