**🛠️ Patch Notes**

**💼 SFJobs — Pick a Job, Get Paid**

A new long-form progression system. Type **/job set <name>** to pick one of five jobs; each has its own GUI (**/alchemist**, **/farmer**, **/miner**, **/lumberjack**, **/hunter**) where you can see your job level, see exactly which items you're allowed to sell, and bulk-sell from your inventory by clicking a tier icon.

- **One job at a time.** Switching wipes your progress in the old one — you'll get a `[Confirm Switch]` chat prompt before anything destructive happens.
- **Sell from inventory.** Click a tier icon in your job GUI to sell every matching item from your inventory at once. Locked tiers show as gray panes with the level they unlock at.
- **Progression matters.** Each job has 7 tiers (6 for Miner). The newest tier you've unlocked is always the most lucrative — both money and xp ramp up with rarity.
- **Need money? /bal** is still your friend — all job earnings go straight to your SFEconomy balance.

> ⚠️ When you switch jobs you lose **all** xp and level progress in the previous one. There's no merge, no refund, no transfer. The chat-link confirmation expires in 30 seconds.

---

**🍷 Alchemist**

Brew and sell potions. Harder brews are worth more.

- **Awkward → Splash of Swiftness → Fire Resistance → Night Vision → Strength → Regeneration → Turtle Master.**
- Each tier matches by **potion type**, not just material — a Strength Potion sells at the Strength price, not the Awkward price. The GUI icons even show the correct potion color so you can tell tiers apart at a glance.

---

**🌾 Farmer**

Grow and sell crops. Easy stuff first, exotic stuff later.

- **Carrot → Cactus → Sweet Berries → Melon → Pumpkin → Chorus Fruit → Pitcher Plant.**

---

**⛏️ Miner**

Sell mineral blocks. The currency-heavy job — iron blocks alone are **$10,000 each** at the cap.

- **Coal Block → Copper Block → Iron Block → Gold Block → Diamond Block → Netherite Block.**

---

**🪓 Lumberjack**

Sell logs. Seven overworld tree species in increasing rarity.

- **Oak → Birch → Spruce → Jungle → Acacia → Dark Oak → Mangrove.**

---

**🗡️ Hunter**

Kill mobs for a chance to drop their head. Sell the heads for money + xp.

- **Zombie → Skeleton → Spider → Zombified Piglin → Enderman → Wither → Warden.**
- **5% chance** per eligible kill. Mobs **from spawners don't drop heads** — no farming spawner traps. Pets and named mobs are also excluded.
- You can collect heads of mobs you haven't unlocked yet; they sit in your inventory until you reach the level that sells them.

---

**🤝 Jobs ↔ /sell**

`/sell` from the shop plays nice with whatever job you're in.

- If a shop sign **and** your own job tier both price an item, `/sell` pays you **the higher of the two** and you still earn the tier's job xp.
- `/sell` **skips items that belong to a job you don't have**. Those need to be sold through that job's GUI, by someone in that job, or not at all.
- Jobless players are unchanged — `/sell` still works at shop prices the way it always did.

---

**💀 Keep Inventory**

Death now keeps your inventory and your xp levels.

- The vanilla **/gamerule keepInventory** is enabled. Items don't drop on death; your hotbar, armor, and offhand stay equipped; xp levels survive the respawn.
- Mob loot, breaking blocks, and any other "loose items in the world" still drop as normal — this only changes what happens when **you** die.
