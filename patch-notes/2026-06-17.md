**🛠️ Patch Notes**

**SFRpg — Skill Rebalance**
- **Fishing:** Passive lure bonus now triggers every 50 levels instead of 100. A Lure 3 rod at fishing level 50 gives effective Lure 4, and Lure 5 at level 100.
- **Treecapitator:** Duration now scales faster — starts at 5s and gains +1s every 10 levels instead of every 100.
- **Dwarf Mode (Mining):** Massively reworked. Now grants a flat +5 Efficiency while active (up from +1). Also adds Fortune — +1 Fortune per 25 levels, capping at +4. A Fortune 3 pickaxe at level 100 becomes Fortune 7 while active. Duration starts at 10s and grows +2s every 25 levels.
- **Drill (Digging):** Reworked. Duration starts at 10s and gains +1s every 10 levels. Efficiency bonus now also scales every 10 levels (+1 per 10 levels).

---

**🍪 New Plugin — Super Foods**
Cookies now do something special. Members and above who eat a cookie get **5 minutes of flight**. Eat another cookie before time runs out and the timer stacks. A countdown warning appears in chat as your fly time runs low. When it expires mid-air, your fly cuts out and you fall — but your first landing is protected. Future falls deal damage as normal.
