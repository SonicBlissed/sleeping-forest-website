**🛠️ Patch Notes**

**⚡ Hoppers — Speed Boost**
- Hoppers now transfer up to **64 items per tick** (previously 1). Item sorting and storage networks will run dramatically faster.

---

**🏰 SFClans — Claim Scaling Rework**
Max claim area per clan level has been rebalanced:
- **Level 1** — 32 × 32
- **Level 2** — 64 × 64
- **Level 3** — 128 × 128

> ⚠️ **Existing clans must re-claim their land for the new sizes to take effect.**

---

**🌍 SFTeleport — /rtp Range Increase**
- The max distance for `/rtp` has been increased from **2,500** to **25,000** blocks. Way more room to find untouched land.

---

**🏰 SFClans — /clan info**
- New `/clan info` command shows your clan's name, level, welcome message, and member list at a glance.
- If your clan owns land, it also highlights the claim borders in glowstone for **7 seconds** so you can see exactly where your territory ends.
