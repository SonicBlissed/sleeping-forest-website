**🛠️ Patch Notes**

**⚒️ SFRpg — Skills Are Now All Passive**

No more sneak + right-click to activate an ability. Every skill rewards you just for *playing the game*.

- **No more cooldowns, no more timer countdowns, no more activation tool.** Whatever tool you happen to be holding, you get the benefit.
- Right-click activation is gone — that input is freed up for other things.

---

**⛏️ Mining — Permanent Haste**

The old Dwarf Mode active buff is replaced by a permanent Haste effect that grows with your Mining level:

- **Level 100** → permanent **Haste I**
- **Level 200** → permanent **Haste II**
- **Level 300** → permanent **Haste III**

The effect is always on as long as you're online and shows in your effects panel. If a beacon is giving you stronger Haste, your beacon wins — we never trample it.

---

**🌳 Logging — Treecapitator on Every Swing**

Treecapitator is now a passive **chance per log** instead of an activated ability:

- **Base 10% chance**, **+1% per Logging level**, capped at **100%**.
- The level cap is gone — keep chopping, keep earning levels, you just stop seeing chance growth once you're guaranteed.
- Every natural log break shows **✦ Treecapitator!** (green) or **✦ Treecapitator missed** (gray) on your action bar so you always know what rolled.
- You need to be holding an **axe** for the roll to fire — otherwise the rest of the tree would chew through whatever tool you happened to be holding.
- **Natural logs now give 2 XP each** (was 1).

> ⚠️ **Treecapitator only fires on naturally generated logs** — logs you placed (or any other player placed) are skipped entirely. We've also made this tracking **persistent across restarts** now, so your log cabin is safe even after a server reboot. Anything placed from this update onward is protected forever. (Logs that *already existed* in the world before this update will still register as "natural" on first encounter — once any player touches them, they're tracked from then on.)

---

**🪏 Digging — Passive Loot from the Ground**

Shovelling natural sand or dirt (including grass, podzol, mycelium, coarse/rooted dirt, and red sand) now has a chance to drop bonus items:

- **Diamond** — 1% base, +0.01% per Digging level, capped at **5%**.
- **Glowstone dust** — 1% base, +0.1% per Digging level, capped at **15%**.
- **Netherite scrap** — 0.5% base, +0.005% per Digging level, capped at **5%**.

Each roll is independent — one break can drop more than one if you're lucky. Only fires on blocks that haven't been touched by a player, same protection as Treecapitator.

---

**🌱 Gardening — Sneak to Grow**

Stand on top of a planted crop, sapling, or **nether wart** and press sneak — there's a chance to bonemeal the plant instantly:

- **Base 10% chance**, **+1% per Gardening level**, capped at **100%**.
- Works on wheat, carrots, potatoes, beetroots, sweet berry bushes, all saplings, and (unlike vanilla bonemeal) **nether wart**.
- Successful grows play the bonemeal sound and particles.
