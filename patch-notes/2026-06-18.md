**🛠️ Patch Notes**

**SFRpg — Quality of Life**
- When a skill's cooldown expires, the action bar now shows **"{Skill} ready!"** in gold — the same spot that displayed the countdown while the ability was active.

---

**🗂️ New Plugin — SFDrops**
- **Wither Skeletons** no longer drop Stone Swords.
- **Iron Golems** no longer drop Poppies.

---

**📦 New Plugin — SFAutoSell**
Two new craftable chests have been added.

**Pandora's Chest** — Craft with 8 Diamond Blocks surrounding a Chest. Place it down and it generates a random item every 5 minutes. Can't be merged into a double chest.

**Auto Sell Chest** — Combine a Pandora's Chest with a **Treasure Hunter** book in an anvil to unlock it. Place it down and any item inserted — by hand or hopper — is automatically sold at the shop's sell price and credited to whoever placed the chest.

---

**⛏️ SFEnchants — New Enchantment: Vein Miner I**
A new custom enchantment for pickaxes has been added.

**How to get it:** Fish with a **Treasure Hunter** rod — same rarity as Telepathy, Zeus, and Slime Rancher books.

**How to apply it:** Combine the Vein Miner I book with any pickaxe in an anvil.

**What it does:** When you break an ore block, every connected ore block of the same type breaks at the same time — up to 64 blocks. Each block is broken as if you personally mined it, so Fortune (including any bonus from Dwarf Mode), Silk Touch, and Telepathy all apply to the entire vein.
