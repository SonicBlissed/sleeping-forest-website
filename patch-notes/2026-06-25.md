**🛠️ Patch Notes**

**⚡ Picky Chunk Hopper — Filterable, Chunk-Wide Pickup**

A new craftable hopper for sorting setups that need more than vanilla can deliver.

- **Recipe**: 8 netherite blocks ringing a vanilla hopper in the middle of the crafting grid.
- **Chunk-wide pickup** — picks up matching items anywhere in its chunk, not just the block directly above.
- **Filter slots** — drop a sample item into any of the right four slots to set what it grabs. Leave them all empty and it grabs anything.
- **Slot 1 is the transfer slot** — the only slot that auto-feeds the container the hopper points at. The four filter slots stay locked: hoppers can't push or pull from them, only your own click can put items in or take them out.
- **Up to 64 items per transfer cycle** — instead of vanilla's 1. Regular hoppers still respect the server's hopper-amount setting; only the picky hopper bypasses it.
- **Plays nice with normal hoppers** — vanilla hoppers above can feed it (filter applies, items only land in slot 1), and vanilla hoppers below can pull from it normally at vanilla speed.

**🌱 Gardening — Sneak to Grow Rework**

The sneak-to-grow passive is bigger and actually reliable now.

- **Grows a 3x3 area around you** on a successful roll instead of just the single block under your feet. No more standing-on-the-sapling awkwardness — stand next to your tree farm, sneak, the whole patch pops.
- **Saplings now reliably grow into trees** on a successful roll. Before, a success could puff the bonemeal particle and leave the sapling sitting there because vanilla saplings only advance ~45% of the time per bonemeal application. The skill now keeps applying until the tree actually generates (or runs out of headroom).
- **Stage-1 saplings are recognized as growable.** Saplings have two stages internally and stage-1 ones were being treated as "already fully grown" and skipped, even though bonemeal can still grow them into a tree from that stage.
- **Nether wart grows fully on success** instead of bumping one stage at a time. The old +1 was technically working but visually almost indistinguishable, which made the skill feel broken on wart farms.

**🪚 SFCraft — /craft Anywhere**

A new command for opening a crafting table without needing the block.

- Type **/craft** to open a vanilla crafting GUI at your current position — works anywhere, no block needed.
- **Inside your own clan's claim**, /craft hooks into the existing clan-chest crafting just like a real crafting table placed inside the claim — recipes that pull from clan chests show up in your recipe book.
- **Inside another clan's claim**, /craft gives you a plain vanilla workbench. The chest-pool only opens up for members of the clan that owns the land you're standing on.

> ⚠️ The clan-chest recipe-book integration now requires clan membership for **all** crafting tables inside claims, not just /craft. If you used to bench-craft inside someone else's claim, you'll only see your own inventory in the recipe book there now.

**✨ Telepathy — Plays Nice with Digging Drops and Treecapitator**

Confirming and tightening up how Telepathy interacts with the new passive skills from yesterday's update.

- **Digging passives** — every bonus drop (**diamond**, **glowstone dust**, **netherite scrap**, and the **$25 cash drop**) goes straight into your inventory when you're holding a Telepathy shovel. The vanilla sand/dirt drop is routed the same way.
- **Treecapitator** — when a Telepathy axe triggers Treecapitator, **the whole tree** routes to your inventory: the origin log, every connected log felled by the chain, and the leaves (including any apple or sapling drops). Fortune and Silk Touch still apply normally.
- If your inventory fills up mid-fell, the overflow drops at the block as a normal item — nothing is lost.
